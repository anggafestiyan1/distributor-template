How Aliases Work

When a distributor uploads a file, the system reads the column headers and normalizes them (lowercase, underscores, no special characters).

A column matches this field if its normalized header equals either:

  • The template's source column for this field, or
  • Any alias listed here

Normalization example:
"Product Name" → product_name
"NAMA BARANG" → nama_barang
